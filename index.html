Phillip Schofield Confirms Rumors

This was not meant to be on television.

He's known for cutting a more composed figure during his hosting duties on This Morning. But Phillip Schofield was clearly in the mood to let loose as he rated different versions of gin on his Snapchat account on Wednesday night.

​

The 55-year-old TV presenter looked in great spirits as he informed fans of the different flavours he was trying out - and proceeded to make a number of hilarious faces along the way.

​

Phillip was seen unwinding in the funny clips, which saw him detailing what to expect before each bottle of gin. 'It's that time of the night and we are sampling gin, ' Phil revealed before trying out the lavender flavour.

However, he clearly wasn't feeling it as he made a number of disgusted faces, before admitting: 'There's flowers in my gin. I don't like flowers in my gin!' Phil also wasn't a fan of cardamom gin but did like the rhubarb flavour.

He then looked into the camera and told his viewers 'I love you' in a slow and scary robot voice. Phillip and his presenting partner Holly Willoughby are set to return to host Dancing On Ice early near year. And judge Jason Gardiner admitted during the show's photocall on Tuesday that he advised the newest crop of celebrities to not overindulge on Christmas. He told The Sun: 'They need to avoid alcohol, any overindulgence on Christmas Day and sex. Absolutely no sex. It drains you of your chi. 'I’m being serious. Like boxers, they will have to abstain. I’m going to.'
